CIRCUIT QUIZ

Is most of your time spent in studying circuits? Is circuit theory your favorite subject? Then this quiz is obviously for you. The quiz deals with basic electronic and electrical components and knowledge about the working of circuits.
Just a bit of Ohm's law, and Kirchhoff’s law and you are done, and Guess what?
The quiz is really interesting for any electronics or electrical enthusiast.

Event Format

The quiz will consist of two rounds:

First Round:

1. There will be 30 Multiple Choice type questions to be solved within 30 minutes.
2. Each question will carry 3 marks.
3. One mark will be deducted on every wrong response.
4. Rough pages will be provided.

5 teams will be short listed from there and the final round will be conducted in the lab.

Second Round

1. There will be 10 different lab problem statements provided and a lottery will be drawn to decide the experimental problem the person is going to conduct.
2. The problem needs a solution in the form of a circuit to be designed using Multisim; the components to be used will be listed in the problem statement itself.
3. The first group to come with a correct solution and a running design in Multisim will be awarded the first place and so on.
4. A maximum of one hour will be provided for the design and implementation of the circuit.
After that if any of the slot i.e. 1st, 2nd, or 3rd is empty, the prize(s) for the remaining slot(s) will be awarded based on the results of the 1st round.

Preliminary ideas of Transistor,AC Model, DC Model, Load Line and OPAMP are Required.

Rules & Regulations

* The coordinator's decisions will be final.
* The use of devices with internet connectivity, repositories of data and copying is    strictly prohibited.
* Use of calculators, electronic watches, or any sort of electronic gadgets is strictly    prohibited.
* Any type of communicative devices will not be entertained inside the event hall.
* Participant found engaging in malpractices will be disqualified on the spot.
