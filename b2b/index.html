Testimonials

This gives me immense pleasure to learn that the Students of the Institute have launched a website under the style 'www.bits2bytes.info' in connection with 'Tech-Fest', being organized by them on September 11 and 12, 2009.
The programme covers a wide spectrum of Co-curricular activities, which include inter-college competitions on Student- Project, Seminars, Debate, Quiz, coding and programming etc.
It's a proven fact that such activities help bringing out latent talents and develop leadership qualities, organizing abilities and confidence amongst the Students.
I wish them grand success.
