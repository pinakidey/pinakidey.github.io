BRAINSTORMS

Well, if you feel that you don’t wanna participate in any of the events, we guess you should think it over once again.
In order to get the visitors involved, sheets containing puzzles and tricky questions like Sudoku, Kakuro etc. will be distributed in the venue itself, periodically.
The first person to solve the sheet correctly will win a prize!!!

Event Format

Registration for this event will be On the Spot.

# The sheets of Brainstorms will be distributed twice on each day of the tech-fest.
# The puzzles will be different on each paper & will be enclosed in an envelope.
# Participants will be given 1 hr to solve the puzzles.
# The first person to solve it accurately is the winner of that session of Brainstorms.
# A person can't take part in more than two session of Brainstorms.

Rules & Regulations

* The coordinator's decisions will be final.
* The use of devices with internet connectivity, repositories of data and copying is    strictly prohibited.
* Use of calculators, electronic watches, or any sort of electronic gadgets is strictly    prohibited.
* Any type of communicative devices will not be entertained inside the event hall.
* Teams found engaging in malpractices will be disqualified on the spot.
