FLAWLESS

Do you love playing with formulas, or solving large equations? Does the beauty of mathematics fascinate you? If yes, then this event will surely win your heart.
The competition is basically a Math MCQ type quiz, each questions having four options, and one is correct.
The level of the questions is the same as mathematics Olympiad competitions, so Gear Up and Happy Solving!!!

Rules & Regulations

1. There will be 30 Multiple Choice type questions to be solved in 1 hour.
2. Each question will carry 3 marks.
3. One mark will be deducted on every wrong response.
4. Use of calculators, electronic watches, or any sort of electronic gadgets is strictly prohibited.
5. Pages will be provided for Rough Works as needed.
6. The coordinator's decisions will be final.
7. The use of devices with internet connectivity, repositories of data and copying is     strictly prohibited.
8. Use of calculators, electronic watches, or any sort of electronic gadgets is strictly     prohibited.
9. Any type of communicative devices will not be entertained inside the event hall.
10. Participant found engaging in malpractices will be disqualified on the spot.
