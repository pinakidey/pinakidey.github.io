CODING ARENA

If you have a zeal for programming, and you are smart enough to play with codes, then Coding Arena is simply made for you.

Event Format

The quiz will consist of two rounds:

Preliminary Round:

1. Each team will comprise of 2 members.
2. There will be 20 Multiple Choice type questions to be solved within 20 minutes.
3. Each question will carry 3 marks.
4. One mark will be deducted on every wrong response.
5. Then 3 questions of coding will be provided which is to solved in 45 minutes time
     limit.
6. Rough pages will be provided.

10 teams will be short listed from there and the final rounds will be conducted on the second day of the Tech-Fest.

Final Round:

1. In each session there will be 13 questions (10 debugging and 3 coding).
2. Each session will be of 1 hr divided into two halves, one for coding, and the other for      debugging.
3. All the candidates will have to do the coding part first. After the completion of the     coding part and its submission, questions for debugging part will be provided.
4. The coding questions are to be solved in 45 minutes time limit and 20 minutes will      be given for 10 debugging problems.
5. To solve the coding problems contestant will be allowed to work on a pre-nstalled
    Bloodshed Dev C++ compiler & editor only.
    This software is distributed under GNU GPL & is freely available from
     http://www.bloodshed.net/dev/devcpp.html

# Participants will be judged on the basis of number of problem solved correctly.
# No marks will be given for an Error-prone solution.
# Although there will be no Negative Marking on the Final Rounds.
# Marks will be given on the basis of solution; better marks for better solution.

Rules & Regulations

* During debugging the monitors must be turned off.

* The coordinator's decisions will be final.
* The use of devices with internet connectivity, repositories of data and copying is    strictly prohibited.
* Use of calculators, electronic watches, or any sort of electronic gadgets is strictly    prohibited.
* Any type of communicative devices will not be entertained inside the event hall.
* Participant found engaging in malpractices will be disqualified on the spot.
