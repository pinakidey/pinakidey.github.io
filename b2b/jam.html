JAM --- Just A Minute --- An Extempore with a difference

Can you think on the feet and speak up? Do you have loads of confidence along with good English language skills? Can you cook up stories instantaneously?
If you possess these qualities then this event is made for you.
The event is designed to test the participant’s presence of mind and alertness, along with the English language skills.

Event Format

1. Each team will consist of two members from the same college.
2. The event will be conducted in two rounds.
3. Initially a member from a team say 'A' is called and is asked to pick up a topic from a bowl of chits; the person is then required to speak on the topic for one minute following the rules given below:

The speaker should not:
a. Make any pause, other than the natural pause of speech.
b. Stammer or repeat words.
c. Speak anything out of the topic, however the speaker can speak sense or nonsense on the topic (with nonsense we mean factually incorrect information).
d. Make any grammatical or pronunciation mistakes during his speech.

4. The job of the other teams is to listen to the speaker and to press the buzzer as soon as the speaker violates any of the rules given above.
The pressing of the buzzer raises an objection, and the person raising the objection must provide a valid reason for the objection raised.
5. The judges will then decide that whether the objection raised, is sustained or overruled.
6. If the objection is sustained, then the speaker will have to go back to his place, and the person raising the objection will continue from the time the previous speaker left speaking.
7. However, if the objection is overruled, then the previous speaker continues speaking and the team raising objection will get negative points.
8. The speaker completing the minute without any error will get full points.

Rules & Regulations

* Only teams of Two Members can participate.
* Students from the same college only are eligible to form a Team & participate in the    Inno-Vision.
* The coordinator's decisions will be final.
* The use of devices with internet connectivity, repositories of data and copying is    strictly prohibited.
* Any type of communicative devices will not be entertained inside the event hall.
* Teams found engaging in malpractices will be disqualified on the spot.
