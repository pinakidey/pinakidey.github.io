INNO-VISION

If you want to feel some heat, and want to get into some hot discussions, with tempers rising, then this event is surely meant for you.
Inno-Vision is the Debate competition, based on the Frank Anthony Memorial style debate, and is one of the most interesting formats.

Event Format

# Each team will consist of two members.
# One person must prepare to speak "for the motion", and the other member is supposed to speak "against the motion."
# There will be three minutes speaking time, and one minute cross questioning rebuttal.
# A member from a team say 'A' needs to speak for the motion initially, and after the speaker finishes speaking, the other member from the same team 'A' will have to speak against the motion. The topic is then passed to the next team and so on.

Rules & Regulations

* Only teams of Two Members can participate.
* Students from the same college only, are eligible to form a Team & participate in the Inno-Vision.
* The coordinator's decisions will be final.
* The use of devices with internet connectivity, repositories of data and copying is    strictly prohibited.
* Any type of communicative devices will not be entertained inside the event hall.
* Teams found engaging in malpractices will be disqualified on the spot.

Topic of The Debate

“In The Realm Of Leadership, It Is Necessary To Appear Good Than To Be Good”
