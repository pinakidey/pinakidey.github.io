JUNKYARD WARS

Have you ever thought of preparing something useful out of trash? Is "Junkyard Wars" your favorite show in TV? If so, then we bring you an opportunity to prove your creativity.

Event Format

# Each team will consist of Two Members.
# Each team will be provided with an equivalennt set of trash (say, ice-cream sticks or drinking straws, glue, pins, rubber bands etc.).
# They are required to make the highest and the most stable structure.
# The structure must be recognisable by sight.
# Structure must have some use.
# Any team satisfying this condition wins the game.
# Maximum 2 hrs will be allowed for this event.
# A short introduction needs to be given to the judges during judging time (preferably of 3 minutes) which must include a few of the structure's uses.
# A list of complete set of materials, to be provided during the event, will be published shortly.

Rules & Regulations

* The contestants will have to bring their identity cards in order to get their registrations done at the venue.
* The model to be made can be prepared before but no instruction manual or procedures(in written or in print )can be carried inside the hall.
* Any team found using unscrupulous means would be disqualified immediately.
* The coordinator's decisions will be final.
* Teams found engaging in malpractices will be disqualified on the spot.
