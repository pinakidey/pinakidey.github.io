PROJECTS

If you are a budding scientist, and can use your talent and innovative skills, in order to build a project which has a high application, then you must not lose this chance to demonstrate your project in front of eminent faculties & professionals.

A project based on any scientific or engineering discipline, which has an application in real-life needs to be developed. The project needs to be a working model of the scientific thought applied, i.e. the project and its description.

The projects will be displayed on both the days of the Tech Fest, and the format followed will be as of a standard science fair.
A Synopsis along with the overall project design needs to be presented along with the project, and the presenter is required to prepare illustrative charts, and other media like a PowerPoint presentation as per their requirements.

Equipments like power supply, CRO, and Function Generators will be provided in the college itself.

On the final day, evaluation of the projects will be made by appointed judges based on the following grounds:
1. The scientific and general application of the project.
2. The simplicity of the project.
3. The originality of the project, and
4. The idea behind the making of the project.
