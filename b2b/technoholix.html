TECHNOHOLIX

Are you a good quizzer, having both general as well as technical knowledge? Well this quiz is meant for enthusiastic engineers, who not only are good in their technical field but also have knowledge of the things going on in the world.
A really interesting one indeed!!!

Event Format

The quiz will comprise of four rounds:

1. A General round consisting of questions related to general knowledge, and current affairs.
2. A Corporate and Technical buzzer round, consisting of questions related to the technical knowledge as well as of the corporate field.
3. An Audio-Visual round consisting of mixed questions, may be general or technical.
4. A Rapid Fire round after choosing a particular topic.
Rules & Regulations

* Only teams of Two Members can participate.
* Students from the same college only are eligible to form a Team & participate in the    Technoholix.
* The coordinator's decisions will be final.
* The use of devices with internet connectivity, repositories of data and copying is    strictly prohibited.
* Any type of communicative devices will not be entertained inside the event hall.
* Teams found engaging in malpractices will be disqualified on the spot.
